CloudSync Pro Enterprise — Product Specification

SKU: CSP-ENT-2024
Annual license: $48,000 per year (up to 500 seats)
Implementation fee: $12,000 one-time (standard deployment, 6-week timeline)

Core features included:
- Real-time document sync across Windows, macOS, and mobile
- AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit
- Admin console with SSO (SAML 2.0, Okta, Azure AD)
- 99.9% uptime SLA with 24/7 email support

NOT included in base tier:
- Dedicated customer success manager (add-on: $8,000/year)
- On-premise deployment (Enterprise Plus only)
- Custom AI workflow automation

Competitive positioning:
- Competitor "FileVault Ultra" advertises lower per-seat pricing but lacks native mobile offline mode.
- Renewal discount: maximum 10% for multi-year commits (approved by VP Sales only).
